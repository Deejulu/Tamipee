Seasonal Variation Tracking

Plan ahead for how each season affects your farm's output.

What is Seasonal Variation? Animals don't produce the same amount all year. In the harmattan, heat stress can drop egg production. In the rainy season, catfish may grow slower. This section lets you record those patterns so you can plan your stock, feed, and pricing ahead of time — rather than being caught off guard. The percentage change you enter is used to generate alerts and recommendations on your analytics dashboard.
