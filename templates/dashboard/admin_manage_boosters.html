Growth Boosters & Supplements

Track every vitamin, probiotic, or supplement given to your animals.

What are Growth Boosters? Growth boosters are any vitamins, probiotics, antibiotics, or supplements you give your animals on top of normal feed. Recording them here lets you track what you spend on supplements per species, and see whether what you're giving is actually improving production. The system stores the expected impact so you can compare it against your real production logs over time.
