About Sample Data

Sample data includes realistic livestock, products, orders, and site content based on Tamipee Integrated Farms actual operations. Use these tools to quickly populate a development or demo environment, or to clear sample data when no longer needed.

  • Populate: Creates categories, species, feeds, boosters, products, orders, testimonials, FAQs, and more.
  • Delete: Removes all sample data created by the populate command, preserving any manually entered data.
